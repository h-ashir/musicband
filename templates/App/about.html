About Harmonyx

Harmonyx, the band with a soundscape built upon elements of folk and classical sounds of India interspersed, at times, with layers of progressive, pop, ambient and electronic textures. 5 years, 250+ shows, 20+ international shows in 10 countries, it truly has been a stupendous journey for the band. With a 10 member ensemble cast the band's strength lies in its versatility and unpredictability thanks to 3 vocalists and seven instrumentalists from the length and breadth of the country, each of whom brings on board, a wealth of diverse musical, linguistic and cultural influences thus creating a unique musical experience that is truly global in nature yet local in idea.

Harmonyx Kochi 2019, the debut show was conducted in 2019 and it has 5 songs in all despite the struggles faced during COVID-19. The second show, was at an Engineering college in Chennai in 2021 , and is a collaborative featuring legendary artists from around the globe.
